Trollbridge Armours The Hero's First Choice

Welcome , welcome to Trollbridge Armours the company that you have inherited from you uncle Frank Abercombe who died in Lagos last year. We are sorry about your loss and that it took us so long to find you. His workshop has manufactured and sold weapons and armours to all kinds of heroes and villains but as your uncle's health went bad the business went down as well.

Your uncle's old staff has left after his death and a year later the workshop will require you to start at zero. There's not much money left as the search for you was very expensive but there's some material in the inventory left and also many manuscripts that will allow you to start the business and gain new customers.

Trollbridge Armours has been created during the 48 hours of the Ludum Dare contest #20 in April/May 2011 by Thomas R. Koll alone. The technology is HTML, SVG (raphaeljs) and a lot of javascript which also includes jQuery and jasmine for writing the more than 80 specs/unit tests. Some of the hero images are taken from openclipart.org but the other graphics are self-made.
